Your Choices

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. We do not sell or share personal information for cross‑context behavioral advertising.

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal data. We keep information only as long as necessary to deliver services and meet legal obligations.
